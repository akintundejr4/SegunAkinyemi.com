About me

Motivated by compassion. Driven by purpose.

There are people in this world whose day to day lives are so interesting, so noteworthy and exciting, that they can make a living by simply recording and uploading their daily activities to media platforms like YouTube. I am not one of those people. I don't consider myself to be a terribly complex individual. If brevity of speech were being rewarded here, I would describe myself as being primarily concerned with making a difference. First in my family, then in my community and by faith one day in the world. In keeping with my desire to be a difference maker, I do the best that I can to balance my time between activities of passion and my professional career. In the process of creating this balance I've found that often times my personal passions overlapped with a certain domain, and specifically one large subset of that domain. That domain is technology, and the subset is software. I enjoy developing and programming software. I have a lot of fun creating and using technologies. As such, I decided to pursue a career involving both.

I am a software developer by trade, but an all-around tech enthusiast at heart. A tech enthusiast, in my eyes, would be someone who loves technology not just for the lucrative opportunities that it brings, but also for its ability to transform lives, careers, companies, markets, industries and entire nations. I dream of becoming a significant part of using technology to change the world.
