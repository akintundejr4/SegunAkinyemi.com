About me

Living with purpose.

If brevity were rewarded when making introductions, I would summarize myself as an individual primarily concerned with making a difference. I'm determined to be a positive influence in my family, city, community and in the world at large. I consider it a part of my life's purpose, to live and work with the goal of leaving a lasting positive impact wherever I am. An important part of this for me is working in an industry that I'm passionate about. Satisfaction in everyday life is a key component of being a light to the world.

Technology, and software specifically, is something I'm very passionate about. Growing up in Seattle probbably influenced me in that direction, but in general, I find myself most at home when a majority of my day involves working with technology. I consider myself a tech enthusiast, which in my view, is someone who loves technology not only for the lucrative opportunities that it brings, but also for its ability to transform lives, careers, companies, markets, industries and entire nations. I dream of becoming a significant part of using technology to change the world.

That's a lifetime goal, but in the interim, I enjoy the day to day art of crafting, creating and problem solving as a software developer. With all that said, I welcome you to my personal website. By exploring you can learn more about my education, projects, interests and work experiences. If you have any questions or would like to speak in more depth, please feel free to reach out via the contact section.
